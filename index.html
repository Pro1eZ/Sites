Features

Everything a serious rider needs.

From your fork's next service interval to next weekend's World Cup schedule — TrailRig keeps it all organised on your phone.

🔧

Garage

Log every part on every bike. Brand, model, serial number, install date, purchase price, and complete service history. Scan QR codes from Fox, Shimano, SRAM and 20+ brands to auto-fill specs.

🔍

Search Parts

Tap the magnifier on any component to instantly search for it across global bike shops. Smart category routing — tap on your fork and land directly in Forks search.

🏁

Live Race Events

Full UCI MTB World Series, Red Bull Hardline, Rampage, Urban MTB, and Crankworx World Tour schedules. Session times shown in your local timezone.

📷

Barcode Scanner

Scan QR codes on Fox, RockShox, Shimano, SRAM products. Model, serial, part number, travel, offset, and axle standard fill in automatically from the brand's own database.

📦

Backup & Restore

Export a complete backup of all bikes, components, photos, and history as a ZIP file. Restore to a new phone in seconds. Photos included.

🔒

Private by Design

All data stored locally on your device. No login, no account, no tracking, no ads. Your garage is completely yours.

⚖️

Weight Tracking PRO

Weigh every component — down to the caliper, rotor and pads — and see your bike's total weight. Set a manual total or let it sum from the parts. Grams or ounces, kilograms or pounds.

🔔

Price Stalker PRO

Watch up to 10 components and get notified when prices drop to your target. Set a percentage discount or exact price alert. Supports any currency.

❤️

Ride Log PRO

Track mileage per bike and import rides directly from Apple Health. Garmin, Wahoo, Strava, Polar and Apple Fitness all supported — duplicates filtered automatically.

☁️

iCloud Sync PRO

Your garage syncs automatically across all your Apple devices via iCloud. Add a component on iPhone, see it instantly on iPad.

About

Built by a rider, for riders.

TrailRig is an independent iOS application developed by a passionate mountain biker who got tired of losing track of service intervals, forgetting which parts are on which bike, and searching through browser tabs for spare parts prices.

The app is free to download with no ads, no tracking, and no account required. All your data stays on your device. Pro features are available as a one-time purchase — no subscriptions, ever.

Revenue is generated through affiliate partnerships with bike shops — when you find and buy a part through TrailRig's Search Parts, a small commission may be earned at no extra cost to you.

Currently in final development ahead of App Store submission. Tested extensively on real devices by active MTB riders. 🤘
