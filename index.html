What TrailRig Does

Everything a serious rider needs.

From your fork's service intervals to next weekend's race schedule — TrailRig keeps it all organised on your phone.

🔧

Garage — Full Component Tracking

Log every part on every bike. Track brand, model, install date, serial number, purchase price, and full service history. Never miss a service interval again.

🔍

Search Parts — Find the Best Price

Tap the magnifier on any component and instantly search for it across top bike shops worldwide. Filtered by category — forks open in Forks, brakes in Brakes.

📅

Events — Live Race Calendar

Full UCI MTB World Series, Red Bull Hardline, Rampage, Urban MTB, and Crankworx World Tour schedules with session times in your local timezone.

📏

Ride Log — Mileage Tracking

Log ride distances per bike to know when components are due for service based on real usage. Import from Apple Health or log manually.

💾

Backup & Restore

Export a full backup of all your bikes, components, photos, and history as a ZIP file. Restore to a new phone in seconds.

🔒

100% Private, No Account

All data stays on your device. No login, no cloud sync, no analytics. Your garage is yours alone.

About

Built by a rider, for riders.

TrailRig is an independent iOS application developed by a passionate mountain biker. The app was built to solve a real problem: keeping track of which components are on which bike, when they were last serviced, and where to find replacements at the best price.

The app is free, contains no advertising, and stores all data locally on your device. Revenue is generated through affiliate partnerships with bike shops and component retailers — when a user finds and purchases a part through TrailRig's Search Parts feature, a small commission may be earned at no cost to the buyer.

TrailRig is currently in final development ahead of App Store submission. The app has been tested extensively on real devices by active MTB riders.
