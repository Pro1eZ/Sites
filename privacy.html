TrailRig

Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

Overview

TrailRig is a local-first iOS application for mountain bike component tracking and management. All data you enter — your profile, bikes, components, and photos — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate servers, do not create user accounts, and do not collect or sell your data.

Data We Collect

We collect only the data you explicitly enter:

  • First name, last name, and nickname
  • Country, city, and optional street address (used only to estimate shipping costs in Search Parts)
  • Bike details, component specifications, and ride distances
  • Photos of your bikes and components (stored locally on your device)

Your location (GPS) is only accessed if you explicitly tap "Use My Location." Only the resolved city and country are saved — the raw GPS coordinate is immediately discarded and never stored.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display your Garage and component history
  • Pre-fill search queries when you look for spare parts
  • Show accurate event times in your local timezone

No data is sent to us or any third party for any purpose other than what you explicitly initiate.

Part Search & Affiliate Links

When you search for a part, your search query (brand, model, spec) is sent to third-party product search services to retrieve results. No personal information is included in these queries. TrailRig may display affiliate product links and may earn a small commission if you make a purchase — this does not affect the price you pay. Each shop's own privacy policy applies to transactions you make there.

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to import ride distances from Apple Health, that data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally. TrailRig requests read-only access to cycling workout data only.

Backup & Restore

You can export a backup of your data as a ZIP file to any cloud storage or location you choose. Once exported, TrailRig has no access to that file. Restoring from a backup replaces your on-device data only.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on-device using Apple's SwiftData framework, which uses the iOS Data Protection API. Your data is encrypted when your device is locked. We use no external databases, no cloud sync, and no remote storage.

In-App Purchases

TrailRig Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple. TrailRig does not collect, store, or have access to any payment information. All purchase transactions are handled by Apple's payment infrastructure and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. Your purchase is permanently tied to your Apple ID.

Tracking

This app does not track you across apps or websites. No App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt is shown because no cross-app tracking occurs.

Analytics & Advertising

This app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising networks, and no crash reporting services that transmit data off-device.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete all data stored by this app at any time. Use Settings → Delete My Account & Data to permanently remove everything. Since all data is stored only on your device, deleting the app also removes all associated data. No request to us is required.

Children (COPPA)

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be included in the next app update and published at the URL in the App Store listing. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via the App Store support link or through in-app feedback.
