Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

TrailRig is an independent iOS application for mountain bike component tracking and management. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data We Collect

TrailRig collects only the data you enter directly:

  • Your name, nickname, and location (city, country) — stored locally on your device only
  • Bike and component details — stored locally on your device only
  • Photos of your bikes and components — stored locally on your device only
  • Ride distances you log manually or import from Apple Health — stored locally on your device only

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking
  • We do not send your data to any server
  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Location

Location is requested once to determine your city and country for showing relevant prices in the Search Parts feature. Your exact GPS coordinates are never stored. You can also enter your location manually.

Apple Health

If you choose to import cycling workouts, TrailRig reads workout distance data from Apple Health. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted.

Search Parts

When you search for parts, the search query is sent to third-party services to retrieve product results. No personal information is included in these searches.

Affiliate Links

TrailRig displays product links from affiliate networks. When you tap "Buy" and make a purchase, the app may earn a small commission. This does not affect the price you pay.

Backup

Backups are created as files on your device and shared to cloud storage of your choosing (iCloud, Google Drive, etc.). TrailRig does not have access to these files once exported.

Support

For support requests, please use the feedback option within the app or contact us via the App Store review section.

Changes

This policy may be updated. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
